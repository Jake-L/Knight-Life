Frequently Asked Questions

What are the controls for this game?

Move your character using the arrow keys and press '1' to jump, '2' for a primary attack, and '3' for a secondary attack. In the future, the key bindings will be fully customizable. If you experience any issues with the controls, keep in mind that most keyboards have a limit to how many keys on a circuit you can press at once. On my laptop's keyboard, it does not recognize three key presses if I hold down two arrow keys and the spacebar at the same time, which is why the default jump button is 'F' instead of the spacebar.

What are the technical specifications required to play this game?

Any modern internet browser, excluding Internet Explorer version 8 or earlier versions.
